THE STAFFING BENEFITS
OF PARTNERING WITH A1 PERSONAL TRAINING

SELECTIVE RECRUITING

A1 Personal Training will aggressively recruit and staff your facilities with top level managers and trainers. Prior to candidates receiving a personal training position they must pass a full background check, provide a current national certification, CPR, and be approved by facility ownership and/or management.

TOP-QUALITY HIRES

A1 Personal Training employees must meet all A1 Personal Training requirements as well as be approved by the gym owner/management. This hiring process ensures that our staff meets your club’s vision and standards.

A TRAINED AND PROFESSIONAL TEAM

A1 Personal Training staff are thoroughly trained and supported by on-site management and monitored through regular performance reviews by A1 Personal Training management.

HEADACHE-FREE

A1 Personal Training handles hiring, vetting, training, scheduling, payroll, insurance and all the other details entailed in staffing and managing a personal training department, freeing gym management to focus on their core business.

A GYM FULL OF GOOD-WILL AMBASSADORS

A1 personal trainers are friendly, enthusiastic and vested. They are focused on providing an excellent client experience and creating a positive atmosphere for all members.

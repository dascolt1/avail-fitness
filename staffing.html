STAFFING

THE STAFFING BENEFITS
OF PARTNERING WITH AVAIL FITNESS

SELECTIVE RECRUITING

Avail Fitness will aggressively recruit and staff your facilities with top level managers and trainers. Prior to candidates receiving a personal training position they must pass a full background check, provide a current national certification, be CPR certified, and be approved by facility ownership and/or management.

TOP-QUALITY HIRES

Avail Fitness employees must meet all Avail Fitness requirements as well as be approved by the gym owner/management. This hiring process ensures that our staff meets your club’s vision and standards.

A TRAINED AND PROFESSIONAL TEAM

Avail Fitness staff are thoroughly trained and supported by on-site management and monitored through regular performance reviews by Avail Fitness management.

HEADACHE-FREE

Avail Fitness handles hiring, vetting, training, scheduling, payroll, insurance and all the other details entailed in staffing and managing a personal training department, freeing gym management to focus on their core business.

A GYM FULL OF GOOD-WILL AMBASSADORS

Avail personal trainers are friendly, enthusiastic and vested. They are focused on providing an excellent client experience and creating a positive atmosphere for all members.

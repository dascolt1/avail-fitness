As a property developer, a building manager, or a residential board member – your ability to support healthy community – orientated lifestyles can differentiate your residential establishment as the premier place to live.

1st Fitness offers fitness and nutrition services at your on-site fitness centers in your apartment complex, condo, or hotel. Complete the atmosphere of your apartments by adding that extra touch that will leave your tenets healthier, more fit, and leading a better lifestyle. Be the dream landlord. When you implement this, you give the residence an opportunity to experience health and fitness right in the comfort of their homes rather than having the additional hassle of going to a gym after a long tiring day at work. It is undeniable that the strength of a community is determined by the participation of those with in it. What 1st Fitness does is to develop a residential fitness service to facilitate comfort to those who don’t feel at ease when it comes to gym trips. Your residence will have the pleasure of getting a fitness trainer visit them right in their community.

Our main aim is to build and grow healthier relationships, meaningful friendships and a happier community whereever we provide our services. Be a part of something great and change lives for the better.

  • Assessment of your health and exercise history
  • Comprehensive review of your fitness goals and knowledge on how to achieve those goals
  • Body fat composition test
  • Resistance training session tailored to your fitness goals and experience level
  • An educational segment that will help to avoid the most common pitfalls that prevent people from achieving their fitness goals
  • A discussion of the personal training programs offered by 1st Fitness

This is our low-cost investment plan, as it is 100% free to your community. Simply let your residence know that we are available, and we will be there for them.
